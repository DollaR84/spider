Help.

Author: Ruslan Dolovanyuk aka DollaR
Version: 1.0

Keys:
Escape - exit;
F1 - help;
F2 - on/off music;
F3 - change bust deck by 1 or by 3 cards;
F4 - change deck 36 or 52 cards;
F5 - new game;
F6 - change level;
Tab - change zone;
Shift+Tab - change zone reverse;
Space - take card or put if card took;
Shift+Space - drop card in house;
